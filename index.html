ABOUT US

A holiday you will remember

Experience the luxury and simplicity of Dar Lavender, enveloped by the scenic allure of Muscat's mountains and a 5-minute drive to Qurum beach. Relax and unwind in the embrace of comfort, traditional Omani and arabic breakfast, and enjoy our lush green garden filled with local palm trees. You will experience life in Muscat like a local in the most prime residential area - walking distance to shops, restaurants, fitness studios and supermarkets. Explore the hidden gems of Muscat, creating memories that linger long after your stay.

Address: Madinat Sultan Qaboos, Muscat, Oman - Oman's most central location
